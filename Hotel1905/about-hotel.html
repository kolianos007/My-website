Об отеле

F ollow in the footsteps of the world’s most glamorous stars and walk along our iconic corridors adorned with precious antiques restored to their original splendor. Impeccably high standards and timeless elegance combine to create our unique five-star hotel in the center of Moscow.

Every inch of this exceptional hotel, just moments from the city’s business center, tells the story of past Moscow glamour but with a fresh luxurious twist. No room or suite are the same and each has its own delightfully unique style. Just like our guests.
